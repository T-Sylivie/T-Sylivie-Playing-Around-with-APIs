How to Use

  1. 1. Type a product name or barcode into the search box, then press Enter or tap Search
  2. 2. Scroll through the list of products and pick the one you are looking for.
  3. 3. Open a product to see ingredients, nutrition facts, allergens, additives, and its nutriscore
  4. 4. Use the information to quickly understand what is inside your food and choose healthier options.
